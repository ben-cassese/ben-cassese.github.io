Welcome!

I'm a researcher in the Caltech GPS department working with Prof. Mike Brown on spectroscopic observations of the Galilean moons.

Using observations by the Hubble Space Telescope, we hope to track spatially resolved spectral features across the surfaces of the four moons, making maps of variations in surface composition.

In the coming months I'll be joining the Columbia Univeristy PhD astronomy program to continue work in planetary astronomy and to begin studies in exoplanet detection/characterization.
